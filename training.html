Rhythm keeping exercise.

The rhythm keeping exercise is like playing drums - You must tap with your foot on a footpedal to generate a pattern of beats within a given time.

Using the foot pedal located under your desk, you will tap out the specified pattern.

The beat pattern is indicated by ones and zeros - ones indicate that you must tap, and zeros indicate that you pause, or withhold your tap until the next beat.

Below is an example. When you are ready to try it, click the button below.

In this case, you must tap your foot twice and then pause once. Give it a try.
